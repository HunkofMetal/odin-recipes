Spaghetti

Description

A tasty dish representative of italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Sauce
  • Pasta

Steps

Brown the hamburger meat in a large pan and drain the fat is desired

Throw in salt, pepper, tomato sauce and paste, water, sugar, basil, oregano, and garlic. Simmer on low for 45 minutes to an hour.

Twenty minutes before the hour is up, cook the box of spaghetti noodles as directed on the package. Once the noodles are cooked, add them to the sauce and serve right away.
